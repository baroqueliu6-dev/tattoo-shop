乐

📖 Historical Context

The character 乐 (lè/yuè) has a dual meaning: happiness/joy (lè) and music (yuè). In ancient China, music was considered essential for cultivating harmony and joy in life.

😊 Multiple Meanings

  • Happiness: A state of joy and contentment
  • Music: The art of sound and melody
  • Pleasure: Enjoyment of life's moments
  • Optimism: A positive outlook on life

🎯 Usage Examples

  • 快乐 (Kuàilè): Happy, joyful
  • 音乐 (Yīnyuè): Music
  • 乐观 (Lèguān): Optimistic
  • 乐趣 (Lèqù): Fun, pleasure

✍️ Stroke Order

Total Strokes: 5 (simplified)

Write from top to bottom.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Pronunciation: Can be read as lè (joy) or yuè (music) depending on context.
  • Placement: Works well on wrist, forearm, or ankle.
