美 (Beauty)

Authentic Chinese Character Tattoo Design - Cultural Meaning Guide

美

📖 Historical Context

The character 美 (měi) represents beauty and elegance. It originally depicted a person wearing elaborate headdress, symbolizing adornment and aesthetic appeal.

🌸 Multiple Meanings

  • Beauty: Aesthetic excellence
  • Elegance: Graceful and refined
  • Beautiful: Pleasing to the senses
  • Perfection: Ideal quality

🎯 Usage Examples

  • 美丽 (Měilì): Beautiful, gorgeous
  • 美好 (Měihǎo): Beautiful, wonderful
  • 美术 (Měishù): Fine arts
  • 美味 (Měiwèi): Delicious (beautiful taste)

✍️ Stroke Order

Total Strokes: 9

Top-bottom structure: 羊 (sheep) on top, 大 (big) on bottom.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Balance: Ensure the top and bottom portions are proportionate.
  • Placement: Works well on shoulder, ribcage, or thigh.
