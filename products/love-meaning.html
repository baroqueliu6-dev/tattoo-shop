📖 The Story Behind the Design

What is love? Maybe everyone has their own answer.

In this design, the top part looks like a hand. It's giving a house — a symbol of protection — to a friend. But who says a friend can only be a person? A friend can be your parents, your kids, your pet, or even a forest.

💕 Eastern Philosophy on Love

Eastern philosophy says: love is not just romance. Love is giving, not taking.

This is the same as the Western saying "Love thy neighbor."
