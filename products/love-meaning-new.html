爱 (Love)

Authentic Chinese Character Tattoo Design - Cultural Meaning Guide

爱

📖 Historical Context

The character 爱 (ài) represents love in its simplified form. The traditional form 愛 contains the radical 心 (heart) in its center, showing that love comes from the heart. The simplified form removed the heart component but retained the meaning.

💕 Multiple Meanings

  • Romantic Love: Deep affection between partners
  • Family Love: The bond between family members
  • Compassion: Love for humanity, charitable feelings
  • Cherish: To treasure something precious

🎯 Usage Examples

  • 我爱你 (Wǒ ài nǐ): "I love you"
  • 爱情 (Àiqíng): Romantic love
  • 爱心 (Àixīn): Compassion, loving heart
  • 可爱 (Kě'ài): Cute, lovely

✍️ Stroke Order

Total Strokes: 10 (simplified)

Write from top to bottom, left to right.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Simplified vs Traditional: 爱 is simplified, 愛 is traditional. Choose based on your preference.
  • Placement: Works well on forearm, shoulder, ribcage, or back.
