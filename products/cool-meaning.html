酷

📖 Historical Context

The character 酷 (kù) originally meant "cruel" or "severe" in classical Chinese. However, in modern usage, it has been adopted as a loanword meaning "cool" in the Western sense - stylish, extraordinary, and impressive.

😎 Multiple Meanings

  • Cool/Stylish: Fashionable and impressive
  • Extraordinary: Standing out from the crowd
  • Bold: Daring and confident
  • Unique: One of a kind

🎯 Usage Examples

  • 太酷了 (Tài kù le): "So cool!"
  • 酷炫 (Kùxuàn): Cool and dazzling
  • 酷哥 (Kùgē): Cool guy

✍️ Stroke Order

Total Strokes: 14

Left-right structure: 酉 (wine radical) on the left, 告 on the right.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Modern Usage: This is a modern meaning. Traditional contexts may interpret it differently.
  • Placement: Works well on arm, chest, or back due to its complex structure.
