史

📖 Historical Context

The character 史 (shǐ) represents history, chronicle, and record. In ancient China, historians held high status as keepers of knowledge and guardians of truth.

📜 Multiple Meanings

  • History: The study of past events
  • Chronicle: A factual written account
  • Legacy: What we leave behind
  • Wisdom: Learning from the past

🎯 Usage Examples

  • 历史 (Lìshǐ): History
  • 史诗 (Shǐshī): Epic
  • 历史学家 (Lìshǐxuéjiā): Historian
  • 青史 (Qīngshǐ): "Blue history" - eternal record of one's deeds

✍️ Stroke Order

Total Strokes: 5

Write from top to bottom.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Similar Characters: Don't confuse with 吏 (official) which looks similar.
  • Placement: Works well on forearm, chest, or back.
