好

📖 Historical Context

The character 好 (hǎo) is composed of two parts: 女 (woman) and 子 (child). In ancient Chinese culture, a woman with a child symbolized goodness, completeness, and harmony.

👍 Multiple Meanings

  • Good: Morally excellent, virtuous
  • Well: In good health or condition
  • Fine: Satisfactory, acceptable
  • Harmony: Balance and peace

🎯 Usage Examples

  • 你好 (Nǐ hǎo): "Hello" (literally "you good")
  • 好人 (Hǎorén): Good person
  • 美好 (Měihǎo): Beautiful, wonderful
  • 好运 (Hǎoyùn): Good luck

✍️ Stroke Order

Total Strokes: 6

Left-right structure: 女 (woman) on the left, 子 (child) on the right.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Simple but Deep: Despite its simplicity, this character carries profound meaning.
  • Placement: Versatile - works well anywhere due to its balanced structure.
