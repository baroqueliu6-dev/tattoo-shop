The Clinical Impact of Integrative Medicine for Surgery Recovery

This approach is not about choosing between Eastern and Western medicine.
It is about combining the best of both worlds.

Top clinical research institutions have successfully integrated TCM with Enhanced Recovery After Surgery (ERAS) protocols to:

  • Shorten hospital stays
  • Reduce severe complications

1. Pain Management & Reduced Opioid Dependency

How it works: Acupuncture stimulates the central nervous system to release natural, non-addictive analgesics like endorphins.

Benefits:

  • Significantly lowers the required dosage of intravenous opioids
  • Cuts off the vicious cycle of painkiller-induced bowel paralysis

2. Accelerated Gastrointestinal Recovery

How it works: Electroacupuncture targeting specific points acts as a "gastrointestinal pacemaker."

Benefits:

  • Dramatically reduces the time patients suffer from post-operative ileus
  • Faster return to normal eating and digestion

3. Chemotherapy Support & Immune Rebuilding

How it works: Therapies utilizing highly active botanical extracts—such as Ganoderma lucidum (灵芝)—combat bone marrow suppression.

Benefits:

  • Reverse multi-drug resistance in tumors
  • Rebuild cellular immunity
  • Better tolerance to chemotherapy
