Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

🛡️ 100% Satisfaction Guarantee

We are committed to providing exceptional service. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll refund your payment in full. No questions asked.

  • Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

2. Service Package Refunds

For Half-Day, Full-Day, and Medical Translation services:

Before Service Starts:

  • More than 24 hours before: Full refund (100%)

After Service Starts:

  • Not satisfied during service: Tell us immediately and we'll stop the service. You'll be refunded for the unused portion.
  • Not satisfied after service: Request refund within 24 hours of service completion. Full refund available if you're not satisfied.

3. Emergency Service Refunds

For emergency services (2-hour response):

  • Once the emergency service has been initiated, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
  • If we're unable to provide the service due to circumstances beyond our control, full refund will be issued.

4. Premium Surcharge Refunds

For premium surcharges (Emergency, Night, Weekend/Holiday):

  • If the premium service is not delivered as promised (e.g., response time exceeded), the surcharge will be fully refunded.
  • The base service fee follows standard refund policy.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: baroqueliu6@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: Refund Request - [Your Name] - [Service Date]
  • Include: Your name, service date, payment confirmation, and reason for refund

6. Refund Processing

  • PayPal Payments: Refunded to your original PayPal account within 3-5 business days.
  • Bank Transfer: If paid via bank transfer, please provide bank details for refund.
  • Currency: Refunds are issued in the same currency as the original payment (USD).

Refunds may be denied in the following cases:

  • No-show without prior cancellation (less than 12 hours notice)

  • Request made after 24 hours of service completion (except for satisfaction guarantee cases)
