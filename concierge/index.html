Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our medical concierge service

🤔 What exactly is a medical concierge?

A medical concierge is a professional healthcare assistant who helps you navigate the medical system. We're not doctors, but we bridge the gap between you and healthcare providers. We help with finding doctors, booking appointments, translating medical information, and accompanying you during visits.

🗣️ Do you provide medical advice?

No, we don't provide medical advice. We're not doctors or nurses. We help you communicate with medical professionals and understand their advice, but all medical decisions should be made in consultation with qualified healthcare providers.

⏰ How quickly can you arrange an appointment?

For non-urgent cases, typically 1-3 business days. For urgent cases, we can often arrange same-day or next-day appointments at hospitals with emergency services. Some specialists may require longer wait times.

💰 Do you accept international health insurance?

We can help you understand your insurance coverage and work with hospitals that accept international insurance. However, payment is typically made at the time of service, and you would file for reimbursement with your insurance provider. We can provide all necessary documentation.

🌙 Do you provide emergency services?

We offer 24/7 support hotline for emergencies. While we can't provide medical care, we can help coordinate ambulance services, direct you to the nearest appropriate hospital, and meet you there to assist with communication and navigation.

👨‍👩‍👧 Do you serve families or only individuals?

We serve both individuals and families. We can assist with appointments for children, elderly parents, or multiple family members. Special rates are available for family packages and ongoing care arrangements.
